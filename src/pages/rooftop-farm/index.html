Sustainability/Exhibit Inquiries

The rooftop urban farm is operated by BlueAcres, the winner of the Impact Enterprise of the year 2024 for their sustainable and autism friendly, vocational training farm.

Farming Inquiries

The farm features farming techniques like Aquaponics, Vertical and urban greenhouse farming. Other interesting setup includes circular features such as edible landscaping and black soldier fly for the reuse food waste. The farm powered by electricity generated by the Solar panels at EXPO and also intend to further minimize its carbon footprint, by selling all fresh produce to the nearby residents and to the F&B of Singapore EXPO.

Visit Information

This farm comprises of wide range of farming system designed for different crop species to demonstrates the vast variety of produce that can be grown locally. Produce available from the farm includes Chili, Winged Beans, Leafy greens, Ginger, Herb and fruit to name a few.

Visit Information

This farm is a destination farm for educators and it promotes volunteerism. Together with Singapore EXPO, the rooftop urban hopes to become one of the most sustainable urban in Singapore.
