BlueAcres'

Grow Rack

Description

These are customed indoor vegetable grow racks designed to cultivate a variety of vegetables and crops hydroponically. These racks are installed with recirculating irrigation and dripped system each simulating growth condition required by the different species. The racks also use different types of LEDs that emits different concentration of lights and the light spectrum necessary for photosynthesis to take place

Description

These racks are designed to demonstrate how a wide variety of nutritious fresh produce can be grown indoor when ideal growth conditions are introduced to the plants. In addition, by going vertical, we can now produce more in the limited space, enhancing our move to becoming more sustainable cultivation and improved nation food security

Harvest Coverage:

  • Strawberries
  • Tomato
  • Rosemary
  • Coriander
  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Batavia Lettuce
  • Xiao Bai Chye
  • Cai Xin
  • Kai Lan
