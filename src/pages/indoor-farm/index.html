BlueAcres' Grow Racks

Constellar, the events and venue management company behind Singapore EXPO, has partnered with Blueacres to revolutionize vertical farming on-site. With expertise from Blueacres and automation innovations by SUTD students, the initiative aims to enhance efficiency, promote local produce, and raise awareness about food sustainability. By championing urban farming, this collaboration seeks to build a more sustainable and secure food future for Singapore.
