Rooftop Farm

Sustainability/Exhibit Inquiries

The farm features farming techniques like Aquaponics, Vertical and urban greenhouse farming. Other interesting setup includes circular features such as edible landscaping and black soldier fly for the reuse food waste. The farm powered by electricity generated by the Solar panels at EXPO and also intend to further minimize its carbon footprint, by selling all fresh produce to the nearby residents and to the F&B of Singapore EXPO.
